How To Make Twitter Painless for Marketing

1. Use Tweetdeck to manage multiple accounts and schedule tweets.
​
2. Tweet during weekly events.

- #screenshotsaturday for in-game screenshots every Saturday
- #wipwednesday for WIPs every Wednesday
- #indiedevhour on Wednesdays at 1PM CST (2AM Tuesdays in MYT)
​
3. Post content that catches the eye in a second or two.

4. Posting at peak time for your audience, usually 11AM CST (12AM MYT).
