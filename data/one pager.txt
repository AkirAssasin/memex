The 'One Page' - a document which shows the main features of your project.

It has to be brief, clean, ordered, attractive, eyecatching and have a visual identity. Keep it simple to send a clear idea and essential information about the game.

Concept: single sentence explanation of the product.
Product info: genre, platform and target audience.
Outline: brief explanation.
Unique selling points.
Ways to learn more about the product.

Configure and modify it to communicate your point effectively. Different audiences and situations may require different elements, so you need to know your audience.
