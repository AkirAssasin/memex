Making the audience feel cold or hot can be achieved more successfully when the audio fully supports the visual effects. In fact often the audio can be more evocative to trigger emotional feelings in the audience than visual changes. 

Some sounds are lush and full of life, others seemed sparse, and some would invoke a feeling of hot, dry, arid landscapes. Some sounds are only heard at dawn, while some only at night. In most instances this is because the sounds were from an appropriate environment and time in the real world.
